Privacy Policy Last Updated: July 12, 2026 Privacy Policy for IRIQ Capture IRIQ Capture ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes, how it is used, and the choices available to you. By using IRIQ Capture, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Process IRIQ Capture is designed to process documents and images primarily on your device. Depending on the features you choose to use, the App may process: Document images Photos captured using the camera PDF documents OCR extracted text AI prompts and responses User preferences Subscription status Optional API keys that you configure 2. Camera Permission The App requests Camera permission solely to allow you to: Scan documents Capture receipts Capture invoices Capture business cards Capture other documents Camera images are processed only for the functionality you request. 3. Photo & File Access The App may request permission to access: Photos Images PDF files Documents stored on your device These permissions are used only to allow you to select files for processing. 4. OCR Processing Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is performed locally on your device using on-device machine learning models. OCR text is not transmitted to our servers as part of the OCR process itself. 5. AI Features IRIQ Capture provides optional AI-powered features such as: AI Summary Ask AI Document explanation Text analysis Content generation AI-based document assistance When you choose to use these features, the content required to generate the response (such as document text and, where applicable, document images) may be securely transmitted to the AI provider you have selected or configured. Examples may include: Anthropic Claude OpenAI Google Gemini Other compatible AI providers supported by the App Your document content is sent only when you explicitly invoke an AI feature. If you do not use AI features, your documents remain on your device. 6. API Keys Some AI providers require you to supply your own API key. User-provided API keys: are stored encrypted on your device; are used only to authenticate requests to the AI provider you selected; are not intentionally uploaded to or stored on our own backend. When you use an AI provider, your API key is transmitted securely to that provider as part of the authentication process required for your request. 7. Local Storage The App stores information locally on your device, including: Documents OCR results AI conversations User preferences Application settings Subscription state Recent activity This information is stored within the application's private storage. 8. Data Retention Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly share or export it. Uninstalling the App normally removes its locally stored data. Android Auto Backup is disabled for this application, so application data is not automatically backed up through Google's Android Auto Backup service. Depending on your device manufacturer or any device-to-device migration tools you choose to use, some data may still be transferred outside the control of this application. 9. Advertising IRIQ Capture may display optional Rewarded Ads. Rewarded Ads are shown only when you choose to watch one. The App does not display unsolicited full-screen ads as part of normal document processing. Advertisements are provided by Google AdMob. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy 10. Subscriptions Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store: Credit card numbers Payment card details Banking information Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. 11. Third-Party Services Depending on the features you use, the App may communicate with: Google Play Billing Google AdMob Anthropic Claude OpenAI Google Gemini Other AI providers selected by the user Each provider maintains its own Privacy Policy. 12. Data Sharing We do not sell your personal information. Document content is shared only when required to provide an AI feature that you explicitly request. 13. Security We take reasonable measures to help protect your information, including: Encrypted storage of supported credentials Secure HTTPS communication Android application sandboxing Android Keystore-backed encryption where applicable No system can guarantee absolute security. 14. Children's Privacy IRIQ Capture is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 15. Crash Reporting and Analytics At the time of this Privacy Policy, Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics are not enabled in the production version of the App. If these services are introduced in a future release, this Privacy Policy will be updated before they become active. 16.
